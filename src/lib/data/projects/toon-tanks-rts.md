### Game Summary

Real time strategy using Mirror Plugin for networking and navigation for pathfinding on click and Steam platform integration.

You can place buildings that will spawn different type of pawns. The main gameloop is to manage to destroy enemy base and defend yours.

Very interesting to play with friends for a quick amusement.
